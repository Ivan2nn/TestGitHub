En Plein Project

Description

The En Plein Project was developed in collaboration with the Laboratory of Bioengineering and the Faculty Of Educational Science of University of "Roma Tre" in Rome. The aim of the project was to design and develop a tool to analyze the cognitive development of the pre-scholar children presenting them games to be played in a virtual interactive environment, created for the Xbox platform.

Languages and Tools

C#, Entity Framework, Kinect SDK

Client

University of Roma Tre, University of Zaragoza
